Brand Color Palette for Suubi (Hospital Web)

Primary Colors
- Dark Blue: #1E3A5F
  - Use for: headers, navbar background, key headings, primary buttons in dark sections
- Green: #2E8B57
  - Use for: success/positive actions, medical icons, outlines, secondary buttons, links
- Orange: #F7941D
  - Use for: CTAs, emphasis highlights, small badges, lifeline accents

Neutrals & Backgrounds
- Light Gray: #F5F7FA
  - Use for: section backgrounds, cards background in light areas
- White: #FFFFFF
  - Use for: cards, surfaces, content areas
- Cool Gray: #A3AAB2
  - Use for: secondary text, captions, dividers

Accent & Highlights
- Teal Tint: #7BC6B3
  - Use for: subtle accents, charts, decorative backgrounds
- Warm Beige: #FFF3E6
  - Use for: CTA section backgrounds, donation emphasis bands

Tailwind/Theming Mapping
- .bg-dark-purple -> now maps to Dark Blue (#1E3A5F)
- .text-dark-purple -> now maps to Dark Blue (#1E3A5F)
- .bg-suubi-green / .text-suubi-green -> Green (#2E8B57)
- .bg-mustard / .text-mustard -> Orange (#F7941D)

CSS Variables (Tailwind theme)
- --primary: Dark Blue (#1E3A5F)
- --secondary: Green (#2E8B57)
- --accent: Orange (#F7941D)
- --background: Light Gray (#F5F7FA)
- --foreground: Cool Gray (#A3AAB2)

Usage Guidelines
- Navigation/Header: Dark Blue background, white text. Hover/active links may use Orange underline or Green text.
- Primary Buttons: Orange on light backgrounds; Green as secondary. On dark blue sections, use white or Orange.
- Icons: Prefer Green for health-related icons; Orange sparingly for emphasis.
- Section Backgrounds: Alternate Light Gray (#F5F7FA) and White. Use Warm Beige (#FFF3E6) behind donation/CTA areas.
- Lifeline Component: Use Orange (#F7941D) to draw attention without overwhelming.
- Text: Headings in Dark Blue; body text in Cool Gray; keep high contrast for accessibility (WCAG AA).

Accessibility Notes
- Ensure contrast ratio >= 4.5:1 for body text. Dark Blue/White and Green/White meet contrast; Orange works best on white/dark blue with bold text.
- Avoid large fields of Orange; reserve for CTAs and small accents.

Examples in Code
- Hero gradient: from Light Gray to faint Green tint
- CTA button: .bg-mustard (Orange) with white text
- Outline button: border .text-suubi-green; hover .bg-suubi-green .text-white
